Core Principles

The five pillars of Sensor Intelligence.

Sensor Physics

Information begins in physical, chemical, and biological interactions.

Intelligent Representation

Raw signals must become meaningful, interpretable, and uncertainty-aware representations.

Adaptive Learning

Sensing systems must learn, recalibrate, and remain reliable in changing environments.

Collective Intelligence

Networks and arrays can generate intelligence beyond individual sensors.

Decision Intelligence

The ultimate purpose of sensing is trustworthy action.
