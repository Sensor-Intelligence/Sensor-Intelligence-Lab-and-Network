The Sensor Intelligence Framework

A closed-loop pathway from physical interaction to intelligent action.

Physical World
↓
Physical Interaction
↓
Sensing
↓
Representation
↓
Inference
↓
Learning
↓
Adaptation
↓
Decision
↓
Knowledge
↓
Action ↺ Physical World

Why a closed loop?

Intelligent sensing is not a linear pipeline. Decisions influence actions, actions modify the physical world, and new interactions generate new observations. Intelligence emerges through continuous feedback between sensing systems and their environments.
