Network

Sensor Intelligence Network

A global collaborative platform for intelligent sensing research, education and translation.

Purpose

The Sensor Intelligence Network connects researchers, students, clinicians, engineers, educators and industry partners working toward intelligent sensing systems for healthcare, environmental monitoring, food safety, sustainability, smart technologies and One Health.

The Network supports collaboration, interdisciplinary training, international partnerships, webinars, research discussions, student engagement and translational opportunities in the emerging field of Sensor Intelligence.
