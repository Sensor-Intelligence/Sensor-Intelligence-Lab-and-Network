Sensor Intelligence Network

A collaborative platform for researchers, students, clinicians, engineers, and industry partners.

Purpose

The Sensor Intelligence Network connects people and institutions working toward intelligent sensing systems for healthcare, environment, food safety, sustainability, and One Health.

Network Activities

  • Collaborative research projects
  • Webinars, workshops, and training programs
  • Student and early-career researcher engagement
  • Industry and translational partnerships
  • Open resources, datasets, and protocols
