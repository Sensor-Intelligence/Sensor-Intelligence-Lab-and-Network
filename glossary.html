Glossary

Key concepts in Sensor Intelligence.

Sensor Intelligence

The science of transforming physical interactions into intelligent, adaptive, and trustworthy decisions.

Sensor Intelligence System

A sensing system capable of representation, learning, adaptation, and decision support.

Sensor Intelligence Loop

The closed-loop process linking physical world, sensing, representation, inference, learning, adaptation, decision, knowledge, and action.

Collective Sensor Intelligence

Intelligence emerging from interacting sensors, multimodal platforms, or distributed sensing networks.

Digital Sensor Twin

A dynamic computational representation of a sensing system and its operating environment.
