Founder

Dr. Vishal Chaudhary

Founder, Sensor Intelligence Lab & Network

Dr. Vishal Chaudhary is a physicist, researcher, and educator working at the intersection of sensor physics, intelligent materials, artificial intelligence, complex systems, and computational intelligence. His research aims to establish Sensor Intelligence as a new interdisciplinary scientific framework that transforms sensing from passive measurement into adaptive, learning, and decision-support systems.

His work spans breathomics, biosensors, nanophotonic sensing, environmental intelligence, digital twins, network science, and One Health technologies, with the vision of developing next-generation intelligent sensing ecosystems capable of addressing global healthcare and sustainability challenges.
