Dr. Vishal Chaudhary is a physicist, researcher and educator working at the intersection of sensor physics, intelligent materials, artificial intelligence, complex systems and computational intelligence.

He founded the Sensor Intelligence Lab & Network to advance sensing beyond passive measurement toward adaptive, learning and decision-support systems for healthcare, environment, sustainability and One Health.
